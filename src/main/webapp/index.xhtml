Welcome to PaperShop!

Welcome to PaperShop! This is the inventory and sales management system for Papelería "Dos Pinos". This application is built with Jakarta EE and PrimeFaces.

The system includes modules for:

  • Product and inventory management
  • Sales processing with automatic IVA calculation
  • Supplier management
  • User and role administration
  • Sales reports and statistics
